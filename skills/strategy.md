# Strategic Frameworks

**Owner**: Strategist
**Purpose**: Provide deep domain expertise and financial context.

## 1. Financial Frameworks
### DuPont Analysis
- **Purpose**: Deconstruct ROE (Return on Equity) into operational and financial drivers.
- **Components**:
    - **Profit Margin** (Operational Efficiency).
    - **Asset Turnover** (Asset Use Efficiency).
    - **Financial Leverage** (Debt Usage).

### CAPM (Capital Asset Pricing Model)
- **Purpose**: Determine required rate of return.
- **Application**: Assessing risk-adjusted returns for new product lines or markets.

### Solvency & Liquidity (Basel III / Solvency II)
- **Purpose**: Assess long-term violation risks.
- **Metrics**: LCR (Liquidity Coverage Ratio), NSFR (Net Stable Funding Ratio).

## 2. Market Regimes
**Purpose**: Contextualize analysis based on macro environment.
- **Inflationary**: High CPI, eroding purchasing power -> Focus on Real (vs Nominal) growth.
- **Recessionary**: Contraction in GDP -> Focus on Cost Optimization and Retention.
- **Expansionary**: Growth phase -> Focus on Acquisition and Market Share.
